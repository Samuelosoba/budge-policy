Delete Your Budge Account

If you wish to delete your Budge account and associated data, follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to budgeappsupport@example.com
  2. Use the subject: “Account Deletion Request”
  3. Include the email address or user ID linked to your Budge account

What Happens Next?

Once we verify your identity, we will delete your account and all related data within 7 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile information
  • All budgeting records
  • Saved preferences and settings

Data Retained

We may retain:

  • Purchase receipts (for tax/legal reasons) – stored securely for up to 6 years
  • Anonymous usage analytics – not tied to any user account

Contact

If you have questions, email us at budgeappsupport@example.com
